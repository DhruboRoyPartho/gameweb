RU Mobile Gaming Carnival

Join the ultimate gaming showdown at Rajshahi University! From November 16-21, 2024, gamers of all levels are invited to compete in the Rajshahi University Mobile Gaming Carnival, organized by ACCE Group and supported by the ICE Programming Club. Battle it out in popular games like Free Fire, FIFA Mobile, eFootball, and Clash of Clans for a chance to win prizes, trophies, and certificates. Open to current and former RU students, with options for solo and team play. Registration is open until November 15, so secure your spot now and prepare for an unforgettable week of gaming!
